About

A small Ontario shop for a big robotics season

JAS Robotics is an independent, founder-run robotics supplier based in Ontario, Canada — built inside the youth robotics community it serves.

The story

Why this shop exists

Anyone who has mentored a robotics team knows the pattern: the part you need most urgently is the one that’s two borders and three weeks away. Competition season doesn’t wait for shipping estimates, and school programs don’t have a procurement department.

JAS Robotics exists to close that gap. We stock the small hardware that seasons actually consume, coordinate group orders so a whole region crosses the border once instead of team by team, arrange pickup around Ontario events, and put together learning kits that make sense for real classrooms and camps.

We’re small on purpose. Season-driven, founder-led, and local — which means when you ask a question, the person answering has fixed a drivetrain on competition morning too.

Honest positioning

What we are — and what we’re not

We are

  • An independent, Ontario-based robotics startup
  • A stocked source for repair packs and consumables
  • A coordinator for consolidated group orders
  • A quote-and-invoice partner for schools and camps
  • People from the Ontario youth robotics community

We’re not

  • A manufacturer
  • A national catalogue or big-box store
  • An official distributor for any brand or competition
  • A discount club — we make no pricing promises
  • A faceless storefront

JAS Robotics is an independent company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official distributor of any vendor or competition organization unless explicitly stated.
