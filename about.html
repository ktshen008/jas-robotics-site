About

An Ontario robotics supplier built inside the community it serves

JAS Robotics supplies parts, kits, and project support for Ontario youth robotics, schools, makerspaces, and student builders — with transparent quoting, school-friendly paperwork, and logistics planned around real build timelines.

Our purpose

Closing the gap between Ontario builders and the parts they need

Anyone who has mentored a robotics team, built a capstone project, or kept a makerspace stocked knows the pattern: the part you need most urgently is the one that’s two borders and three weeks away. Competition season, class deadlines, and lab schedules don’t wait for shipping estimates.

JAS Robotics exists to close that gap. We prepare packs of the small hardware that builds actually consume, coordinate group orders so a whole region crosses the border once instead of order by order, arrange pickup around Ontario events, and put together learning kits that make sense for real classrooms, camps, and project spaces.

We’re deliberately focused: competition consumables, consolidated importing, program kits, and parts-list support for serious student and maker projects — a scope we can stand behind completely. And because JAS is founder-led, accountability is direct: the person answering your question has fixed a drivetrain on competition morning too.

Honest positioning

What we are — and what we’re not

We are

  • An independent, Ontario-based robotics supplier
  • Assemblers of season repair packs and consumables
  • A coordinator for consolidated, cross-vendor group orders
  • Quote, purchase-order, and invoice support for schools, camps, and organized project spaces
  • People who build and support Ontario robotics projects

We’re not

  • A ten-thousand-SKU catalogue — we focus on depth in what Ontario seasons actually consume
  • Tied to any single vendor — we source across suppliers to fit your build
  • A discount scheme — estimates are honest, and landed cost is confirmed before you commit
  • A faceless storefront — one accountable person, from quote to handoff

JAS Robotics is an independent company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official distributor of any vendor or competition organization unless explicitly stated.

For teams, schools & projects

Working with us

  • Transparent quoting

    Landed cost — exchange, shipping, customs, and taxes — is itemized and confirmed with you before any order is placed.

  • School-friendly paperwork

    Quotes, purchase orders, and invoicing that fit institutional purchasing — no credit card required.

  • Compliance-first operations

    Written sourcing and content standards: honest condition labels on used parts, safety checks before kits go on sale, and no claims we can’t back up.

  • Local logistics

    Consolidated importing and arranged handoff on the Ontario competition and school calendar.
