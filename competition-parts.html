Everybot BOM support

Building the FRC Everybot, the FTC Everybot, or another published robot design? Send us the official BOM or parts list for your season, your team number, your event timeline, and any parts you already own.

We’ll turn the list into an Ontario sourcing plan: vendor availability, Canadian and local equivalents where appropriate, notes on 3D-printed and custom parts that can’t be purchased, timing, and confirmed landed cost. Larger lists can be folded into a consolidated group-order round with local handoff.

Everybot is a third-party community robot resource. JAS Robotics is not affiliated with or endorsed by its authors, and the official Everybot documentation remains the source of truth for design, assembly, compatibility, and in-season updates.

Used & surplus

Individually listed used and surplus parts, each with an honest condition label — a budget-friendly way to stock a practice bot or a spares bin.

Every listing carries one of four condition labels:

  • New surplus
  • Lightly used
  • Tested used
  • For parts only

Used and surplus parts are available as-is unless otherwise stated.

Current listings are coming soon. Looking for something specific? Ask — we may have it on the shelf.
