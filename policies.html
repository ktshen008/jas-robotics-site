Group order terms

  • All amounts are estimates until final landed cost is confirmed. Final cost may include exchange rate, shipping, customs, duties, brokerage, HST, and handling.
  • Consolidated orders are placed only after participant confirmation. Cancellations may not be possible after the consolidated order is submitted.
  • Manufacturer warranty, where applicable, remains subject to the original vendor's policies.
  • JAS Robotics is not responsible for vendor delays, discontinued parts, customs delays, or competition-rule changes.
  • Payment is by e-transfer or invoice after confirmation. No payment is collected through this website for group orders.
