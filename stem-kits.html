The difference

Why get kits from a robotics shop?

  • Chosen by program people

    We pick kits we’d run in our own programs — buildable, teachable, and durable enough for real student hands.

  • Backed by a parts shop

    The same shop that supplies competition teams stands behind the kits — support and replacement parts come from people who fix robots.

  • Ready for the school year

    Quotes, invoicing, and delivery planned around terms and camp sessions — not around a checkout cart.

  • Safety-checked first

    Kits go on sale only after import, safety, and support checks clear — no shortcuts on gear headed to young students.
